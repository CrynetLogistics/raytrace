Although there are some minor details I have yet to address, the vast majority of the features have been implemented. These include a variety of materials and shapes, support for texture mapping, soft shadows, global illumination, GPU acceleration and adaptive multisampling. Rayte now also has a parser to import .obj files created by your favourite 3D editing tool.
